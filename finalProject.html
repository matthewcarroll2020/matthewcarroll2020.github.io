Hello everyone and welcome to my game, Fishy Bomb. In this game the goal is too score as many points as possible before the 60 second timer runs out. You are on the open ocean and are fishing for mahi-mahi (1 point) and tuna (3 points). The mahi-mahi are abundant and slower compared to the faster and less abundant tuna. However, you must watch out for the dangerous bombs that are among the fish. If you hit your hook on a bomb three times you automatically lose the game! Try to score as high as possible and enjoy playing :).
